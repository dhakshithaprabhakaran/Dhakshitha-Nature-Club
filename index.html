What is Sustainability?

Simple idea

Sustainability means using nature carefully so people, animals, and plants can be happy now and also in the future.

Why it matters

Our outdoor spaces (parks, rivers, trails) are like nature's playground. If we litter or waste water, they get sad. If we take care, they stay awesome!

Easy rules

  • Reduce 🔽
  • Reuse 🔁
  • Recycle ♻️

Earth Day Highlights 🌍

Planting trees helps shade parks, clean air, and make homes for birds.
